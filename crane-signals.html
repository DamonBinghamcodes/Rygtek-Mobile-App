Crane Signals

Reference guide for standard crane hand signals and whistle commands.

Hoisting Up / Raise

Hand Signal: Arm raised, index finger pointing upward, small circle motion

Whistle/Buzzer: 2 Short Blasts

Luffing Boom Up

Hand Signal: Thumb upward motion

Whistle/Buzzer: 3 Short Blasts

Slewing Right

Hand Signal: Arm outstretched, pointing right

Whistle/Buzzer: 1 Long, 2 Short

Slewing Left

Hand Signal: Arm outstretched, pointing left

Whistle/Buzzer: 1 Long, 1 Short

Jib-Trolley Out (Telescoping Boom Extended)

Hand Signal: Both hands pushing outward

Whistle/Buzzer: 1 Long, 3 Short

Jib-Trolley In (Telescoping Boom Retract)

Hand Signal: Both hands pulling inward

Whistle/Buzzer: 1 Long, 4 Short

Travel and Traverse

Hand Signal: Arm swinging horizontally

Whistle/Buzzer: Not Applicable

STOP

Hand Signal: Arm raised, palm open facing crane

Whistle/Buzzer: 1 Short Blast
