Crane Signals

Standard hand signals used during lifting operations

Rope Up

Raise hand in a circular motion, speed of rotation indicates hoist speed.

Rope Down

Arm extended downward, hand pointing down, circular motion.

Stop

Palm or first raised towards the operator.

Slew left or right

Arm extended, point in direction of slew.
